Art Project Two

This was part of my Art Installation project. We grebbed a few words out of a basket and created a powerful saying. After creating our saying, we recorded it and got our sound wave. We then drew our sound wave and then transfered it onto a piece of wood. I then painted it using colors I thought were approprite. My saying was "Love is anger and suffering".
