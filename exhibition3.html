Art Project Two

This was part of my Art Installation project. We grabbed a few words out of a basket and created a powerful saying. After creating our saying, we recorded it and got our sound wave. We then drew our sound wave and then transfered it onto a piece of wood. I then painted it using colors I thought were approprite. My saying was "Love is anger and suffering".

This is my shoe that I created in my World Studies class. It was related to The Holocaust. We created a character who either survived or died during the Holocaust. My character was a 15 year old girl. She had a mother, father, and two brothers. She loved music and nature. Her favorite time of the day was night. After creating our character, we had to paint the traits on a shoe.

For Chemistry, each student chose an element. My element was Arsenic,a metaloid. We had to design our element, I chose a skull and cross bones to show that it is toxic. The "As" is the atomic symbol. It is supposed to represent the element at a melting point. The "33" at the top right corner is the atomic number. The number on the cross bones is the atomic mass.
